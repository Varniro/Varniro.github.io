About Us

UCL is a small effort by a few freshers and Cricket Enthusiasts, Unofficial Cricket League was first envisioned as a get-together and get to know each other event for a lot of freshers of different branches who had previously never interacted with each other due to Online classes, But now it had also turned into a stunning event of sportsmanship and awesome rivalries!
