Star Performances

Abhijeet Kureriya (EC)

Civil vs EC

Abhijeet, rightly nicknamed "Jaddu" took 4 wickets in one innings, beside his amazing bowling skils, he is a great batsman and shows awesome sportsmanship.

Raj Patel (IOT)

IOT vs Mech

Raj Played a mind-blowing match when he scored 52 runs in just 13 balls. Quickly gaining fame for his amazing feat, Raj who is also an awesome sportsman in general has shown fabulous teamwork.

Adesh Raj (Civil)

Civil vs Mech

Adesh threw perhaps the best over of the tournament yet, giving only 4 runs while taking 3 wickets in the same over and 4 wickets in total in one innings.

Pranjal Dubey (Civil)

Civil vs IOT

Pranjal scored an awe-striking amount of 52 runs in just 21 balls during a high critical condition in the match, while greatly contributing to the high target set by the team.

Aman Shukla (Mech)

Civil vs Mech

Aman hit a great 51 runs during a high profile match, This great performance helped his team greatly in the tournament.

Pradeep Raghuwanshi (AIR)

AIR vs MAC

Pradeep had everyone looking fumbled when he hit his great 54 runs in a matter of a couple of overs taking his team to an easy win.
